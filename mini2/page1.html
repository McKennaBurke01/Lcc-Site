Christian Dior.

Christian Dior was a French fashion designer known for creating one of the most known fashion houses in the world. Coming from almost nothing, Dior became well known for his use of sintched waistlines, big skirts and interesting use of textiles. These characteristics helped lead Dior to the top of the fashion tier. Some would even say he was "fashion" in itself.
