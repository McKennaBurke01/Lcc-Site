Alexander McQueen.

Alexander McQueen, was an iconic fashion designer. What was so unique about McQueen was how short lived his fashion career was and how easily his designs captivated audiences. If you look up any of his designs your eyes are instantly draw to his unique usage of tailoring to create breathtaking pieces. McQueen was also very well knwon for his fashion shows as a whole, he had a gift of knowing what music to use, what lighting, hair, makeup and more to truly draw the audience fully in. Alexander McQueen was truly a fashion icon whose name will live on forever.
