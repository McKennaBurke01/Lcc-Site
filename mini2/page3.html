Coco Chanel.

Coco Chanel,not much to explain about who this is, assuming most of you have heard of Chanel before. Coco Chanel was among one of the top French designers of the post World War I era. Chanel took it upon herself to help liberate women of all types from the constraints of the typical corsete fashion to a more sporty,casual look freeing women from societies standards placed on them.She was also very well known for her "little Black Dress" which is what is shown in the image below.
