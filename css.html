Why is Fashion so important?

Fashion is more then just a piece of clothing. It is something that allows creative exploration and can be used as a form of therapy for some people. Below is a list of other ways fashion is more then just fabrics and thread.

  • Relieves stress and anxiety
  • Changes beauty standards
  • Helps create a larger form of imagination
  • Opens your mind
  • Helps improve confidence
  • Brings people together
  • Helps explore and encourage self expression
